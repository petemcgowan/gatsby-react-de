Underground Funk – 70s Style, Breaks Vibe

Underground Funk – All party zone funk, mostly from the 70s, with a modern Stones Throw like twist. Laid back weekends, fire hydrants in the heat, the bump and the bounce. EP02 – host: Diplomatic Enjoy.

More in this mix at https://diplomaticenjoy.com, the Underground Funk playlist, https://instagram.com/diplomaticenjoy.
